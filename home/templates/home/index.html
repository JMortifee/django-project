About Me

I am a secondary school art teacher, printmaker and painter. I studied Drawing and Print at the University of the West of England UWE. Originally from Burton on Trent, Staffordshire I moved to Bristol in 2016 for University. My focus lies with monochromatic portraits in the disciplines of painting, drawing and relief printing. Recently I have been using more colours in my paintings all of which are created using the zorn pallete. I work on sizes from A5 to 4ft by 4ft and have ambition to work larger.
